A great underdoing. Ergle isn't a project management app; it isn't a file sharing app or a messaging app. It is all the things they have in common. But NOT everything each does on its own. It's for sharing what is happening in all those apps you use for their advanced specific functions. And sending a message, sharing a file and looking at project progress when you don't need those advanced functions.

You can see the status of projects, access shared files and send messages, because completing a piece of work (by e.g. creating a document), adding a file to dropbox or google drive and letting everyone in your team know the contents of your conversation with the client are all events that happen in your work life, which you can share with others.

Ergle is a repository of events in your work life. Each event contains the data necessary to access the object it relates to (e.g. message, file, whatever) so you can see the details of what's happening direct from your, your colleague's or your project's timeline. Comment on all events; make them visible to the rest of your company (without, for example, sending everyone in your company an FYI email) or the client themselves.

Ergle Apps will allow you to see recorded event data in a load of ways. Want to see what happened in the form of a calender along with events scheduled for the future? The Ergle calendar app. What about generating timesheets automatically from the record of all the things you did this week? Ergle timesheet app. Develop your own apps available just to your company, e.g. report on how client interactions across the whole company correlate with invoices submitted.

Ergle is Open. You need only have access to an email account something was shared to. No registration required. Just click the link and see the content. And Ergle will never market to emails used just for sharing content - so you'll never be signing someone up to spam by sharing something with them in Ergle.

Ergle is secure. Best practice encryption, security and authentication mean you can trust Ergle to share what you want to share and keep private what you don't.

Ergle is the substrate for a new way of working. Everything everyone does will be tagged with who did it, when it happened and what it related to (i.e. #tagged). Each event can be visible only to its originator, or whomever she chooses. Brought to the attention of others or not. Accessible regardless of location. Browsed. Commented on. Reported.
