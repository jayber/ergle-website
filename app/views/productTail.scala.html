Are you the bottleneck?

 

Email and file systems are dead-ends for operational information.

Email hits your inbox like a rock down a well until you come along and deal with it. This means that:

  1. no none else can act and
  2. you have to.

Hard drives are the same. You manually share each file. Shared file systems are no better: who knows where to find anything without asking?

An intranet is another place to hide information. People find things the only way they know how: they email someone - back to paragraph 1.

Release the genie

We use lots of systems. That's great: they all do different things.

But we need one place that reflects the work we do everywhere, allowing us to share it automatically and securely, with just one place to look.
