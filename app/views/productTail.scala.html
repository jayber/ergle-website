One place

You use lots of systems. That's great: they all do different things.

Ergle is one place that reflects the work you do everywhere

  • to share it automatically and securely
  • with just one place to look.
 

Are you the bottleneck?

 

Email and file systems are dead-ends for operational information.

Email drops into your inbox like a rock down a well. So:

  1. no one else can act and
  2. you have to.

Hard drives are the same. You manually share each file. Shared file systems and intranets are no better: who knows where to find anything without asking?

People search by emailing someone - back to where we started.

Free yourself

Take what you know about what to share with whom and give it to a system that does all the sharing for you.

The system monitors emails, local and shared file, web, mobile and your own systems to update the events stream - applying sharing and visibility settings that you've defined.
