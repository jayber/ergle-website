People

I am James Bromley, the founder and currently the only person working full time on ergle.com. Take a look at my blog to see how I got here. In short, I used to work for other people, now I've decided to work for myself, with a strong desire to help others work as fulfillingly as possible. Ergle is both a product that makes communicating about work easier and a (proto-) company that will embody the principle of profitable fulfilling work. My background is technical, my last job being a software architect for Thomson Reuters. I have a BSc in Psychology and an MSc in Computer Science.

I've always been frustrated that companies don't use the best knowledge available to manage people and make decisions. It seems that business is somehow still stuck in a dark age of alchemists, visionaries and charlatans. On the other hand there is a burgeoning body of literature and research on how to get great results without relying on hunches and management "styles", but evidence and reason.
