the future of work

Imagine building a house. Anyone passing by can see you are building a house, how far you have got and whether you are doing a good job.

If you are working in the digital realm none of this is true. It's like each of us goes down a hole to work each day.

Ergle will change that, making the invisible visible and doing into saying .

More specifically: Let everyone in your team know what you are doing and find out what they are doing, without the burden of emailing and uploading. Show activity from all your channels in one place.

How: Integrate with all your work software to show a timeline of all that happens in your work life, allowing you to share entries with anyone and choose who can see them on your timeline.
